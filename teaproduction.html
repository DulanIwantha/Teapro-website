Tea Cultivation.... 



How to Plant Tea in 7 Steps Buy tea seeds. 

 1.Find tea seeds online or at your local nursery. 

 2.Prepare your soil. 

 3.Tea thrives in sandy and acidic soil with a pH between 5.5 and 6.5. 

 4.Soak and dry out your seeds. 

 5.Nurture your seeds. 

 6.Plant your tea. 

 7.Water your tea plant every day. 

 8.Allow your plant to grow.
